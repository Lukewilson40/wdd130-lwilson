What exercises should I do?

    At home:

  • Bodyweight exercises such as push-ups, squats, lunges, and sit-ups. Jumping jacks, high knees, and other cardio exercises.
  • Yoga or Pilates.
  • Resistance band workouts.
  • Dancing to your favorite music.
  • Stairs workout or using a stationary bike or treadmill.

    • Outside:

  • Brisk walking or jogging or Running or sprinting.
  • Hiking or trail running.
  • Biking or Roller blading.
  • Swimming or water aerobics.
  • Playing sports like soccer, basketball, tennis or frisbee.
    • Remember, before starting any exercise program, consult with a medical professional if you have any concerns about your health or physical abilities.

How should I maintain and active lifestyle?

    Maintaining an active lifestyle is important for overall health and well-being.

  • Set realistic and achievable goals for yourself. Start small and gradually increase your activity level.
  • Choose activities that you enjoy doing, so you are more likely to stick to them in the long term.
  • Schedule your workouts or physical activities into your daily routine to make them a habit.
  • Vary your activities to prevent boredom and to challenge your body in different ways.
  • Involve friends, family, or join a group to make physical activity social and enjoyable.
  • Prioritize physical activity as part of your daily routine, just like eating and sleeping.
  • Take short breaks from sitting, stretch and walk around if you work a sedentary job.
  • Use apps or journals to track your progress, it will help you to see improvements and motivate you to keep going.

    • Consult with a medical professional before starting any new exercise program, especially if you have any health concerns or physical limitations.

What should I do at a Gym?

    There are many exercises you can do at a weightlifting gym, and the specific exercises you should do depend on your fitness goals and current fitness level.

  • Squats - Targets quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings
  • Deadlifts - Targets back, glutes, and hamstrings
  • Bench press - Targets chest, triceps, and shoulders
  • Shoulder press - Targets shoulders and triceps
  • Pull-ups/chin-ups - Targets back, biceps, and shoulders
  • Dips - Targets chest, triceps, and shoulders
  • Rows - Targets back, biceps, and shoulders
  • Bicep curls - Targets biceps
  • Tricep extensions - Targets triceps
  • Leg press - Targets quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings

    • Exercises can also engage secondary muscles as well, and proper form and technique are crucial to avoid injury and get the most out of your workout. It's also important to incorporate a variety of exercises to target all muscle groups.

Why should I maintain an active lifestyle?

    Maintaining a healthy lifestyle can have numerous benefits for your physical and mental well-being.

  • Maintaining a healthy lifestyle can reduce your risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, stroke, diabetes, and some cancers.
  • Exercise and a healthy diet can improve your mood, reduce anxiety and depression, and help you cope with stress.
  • Regular physical activity and a healthy diet can help you sleep better, which is essential for overall health and well-being.
  • Maintaining a healthy weight can reduce your risk of chronic diseases, improve your overall health, and boost your self-esteem.
  • A healthy lifestyle can boost your immune system, making it easier for your body to fight off infections and illnesses.
  • A healthy lifestyle can increase your life expectancy and help you live a longer, healthier life.

    • Maintaining a healthy lifestyle can help you feel and function better, physically and mentally. It's never too late to make positive changes in your lifestyle.
